Frequently Asked Questions

Is VaultDL completely free?

Yes. VaultDL runs on Cloudflare Workers' free tier and will always be free to use.

What video quality is available?

We support 360p, 720p HD, and 1080p Full HD depending on what the source platform offers.

Can I download private videos?

No. VaultDL can only access publicly available videos. Private, members-only, or geo-restricted content cannot be downloaded.

How does the trimming feature work?

Enter a start time (e.g. 1:30) and end time (e.g. 2:45) before downloading. The server will extract only that segment.

Are downloaded videos stored on your servers?

No. Files are streamed directly to your browser via Cloudflare edge nodes. Nothing is stored.

Is it legal to download videos?

Downloading copyrighted content without permission may violate terms of service and local laws. Only download videos you own or have explicit permission to use.
